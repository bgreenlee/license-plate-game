How to Play:

  • Each license plate shows three letters and some irrelevant numbers.
  • Enter a valid English word that contains those three letters in order (they don't need to be consecutive).
  • Your words must be at least four letters long.
  • Shorter words earn more points!
  • You have 60 seconds to find as many words as possible.
  • You can skip a word, but you'll incur a penalty.
  • Example: For "CPY", "capybara" works, but "copy" is better because it's shorter.
